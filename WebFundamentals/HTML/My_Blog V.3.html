Xenogears Game Facts

Xenogears was one of the best games for the Playstation 1. Featuring a mind-boggling story of unparallelled depth and a plethora of unforgettable characters, Xenogears is a graphic novel in video game form that everyone who calls himself/herself a gamer should play.

List of Playable Characters

  • Fei
  • Citan
  • Elly
  • Bart
  • Rico
  • Billy
  • Maria
  • Chu-Chu

  5. Replay Value
